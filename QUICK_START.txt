## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Do I have to upload a picture?**  
A: No! Choose manual entry if you prefer typing.

**Q: How does AI know the location?**  
A: You click on map, enter coordinates, or describe it.

**Q: Can AI analyze any photo?**  
A: Yes, JPG, PNG, BMP formats. Analyzes issue in image.

**Q: Is satellite verification free?**  
A: Optional feature using your Gemini API key.

**Q: How do I track my complaint?**  
A: Use tracking ID on "Track Complaint" page.

**Q: Can I edit after submitting?**  
A: No, but can create new complaint if needed.
